PHM Agent in action: Intelligent maintenance decision-making powered by Large Language Models for industrial equipment health management.

Abstract

We introduce PHM Agent, an intelligent maintenance assistant that leverages Large Language Models to revolutionize prognostic health management. Our agent combines advanced AI reasoning with domain expertise to provide automated fault diagnosis, predictive maintenance scheduling, and intelligent maintenance recommendations. Through natural language interaction, maintenance teams can query equipment health, receive predictive alerts, and access actionable maintenance guidance. The agent integrates real-time sensor data, maintenance history, and expert knowledge to deliver context-aware maintenance decisions, reducing downtime by 35% and maintenance costs by 28% across industrial deployments.
